About Me

My name is Omar and I grew up in Mosul, Iraq. Due to the war, I moved to Erbil where I now live with my wife and baby girl. I graduated from the Institute of Technology in Mosul from the Department of Surveyance. After graduation, I worked for numerous oil companies in Iraq. In my spare time, I like to read novels (The Merchant of Venice is one of my favorites) and work on becoming an awesome programmer.

My Life Before Re:coded

Before I joined Re:coded I was working in an oil company in the city of Kirkuk. After the occupation of vast areas of my country, including my city and my place of my work, I moved with my family to the city of Erbil. I began to seek work but there was no vacancies because of the war. I found out about the Re:Coded program, and decided to apply.

Re:coded

I joined the program because of my desire to get a job in the local or global market. Through my studies, I found that the world of programming is very big and through which one can develop his skills, keep pace with technology and turn his dreams into reality, using simple resources.

My Goal After Re:coded

I am very keen to practice what I learned from the program, continue to develop my skills in programming and learn new languages. I hope that my land will be liberated, and I can go back to my city and my business as soon as possible. I also hope that I can develop my work by taking advantage of what I learned from the program and turning my dreams into reality.
